## 1. Purpose
Build a serious, utility-first, decentralized cryptocurrency and smart-contract ecosystem that lets parents/guardians create long-term, compounding wealth accounts for children. The protocol combines voluntary savings, transparent yield mechanics, timed payouts, and a real-world retail discount network (FTC Stores) to reduce generational poverty.

## 2. North Star Principles
1. **Utility over hype:** Every token feature must map to a real family benefit.
2. **Compliance-first:** Treat U.S. and global securities/commodities law as a design constraint, not an afterthought.
3. **Sustainability over guarantees:** Yield and discounts are funded by measurable protocol cash flows (fees, store margins, treasury returns), not by later investors.
4. **Open and auditable:** All core logic is on-chain, open-source, and upgrade-governed.
