# FTC Community Launch Playbook

**Goal:** Build a fan base of 10,000+ families before token deployment.  
**Timeline:** 60–90 days.  
**Brand Promise:** *"The first crypto built to end generational poverty — one child at a time."*

---

## 1. Audience

| Segment | Why They Care | Where They Are |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Young parents 25–40 | Want to set kids up financially | TikTok, Instagram, Facebook groups |
| Crypto-curious skeptics | Burned by memecoins, want utility | Twitter/X, Reddit r/CryptoCurrency |
| Low-income families | $250 + $500 match is real money | Community centers, churches, local nonprofits |
| Black & Latino communities | Generational wealth gap is personal | Twitter/X Spaces, Instagram, WhatsApp |
| Teachers / coaches / pastors | Already serve children | Email lists, local events |
| Foster / adoptive parents | Extra motivated to build stability | Facebook groups, nonprofit networks |

---

## 2. Messaging Pillars

1. **Emotion:** Every parent wants their child to have more than they did.
2. **Utility:** Real discounts at real stores for real families.
3. **Fairness:** Low-income families start with $500, not $250.
4. **Transparency:** Open-source. On-chain. No mystery.
5. **Legacy:** This is a 25-year wealth-building system, not a flip.

---

## 3. Content Engine

### Weekly Cadence
- **3 short videos/week** (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- **5 tweets/day** from founder account
- **1 long-form thread/week** explaining tokenomics
- **1 Twitter/X Space/week** with parents, educators, crypto builders
- **1 email/week** to waitlist

### Video Hooks
- "I put $250 into crypto for my baby. In 25 years, it could pay for college."
- "Walmart accepts this coin and parents get 30% off diapers."
- "The government won’t save your kids. But FTC might."
- "POV: your child turns 25 and the account you forgot about is worth $50,000."
- "Rich families trust funds. Poor families get FTC."

---

## 4. Viral Mechanics

### Waitlist + Referral Program
- Join waitlist with email.
- Get unique referral link.
- Earn **early allocation tiers**:
  - 5 referrals → 1,000 FTC
  - 25 referrals → 10,000 FTC + "Founding Parent" badge
  - 100 referrals → 50,000 FTC + merch + invite to private founder channel

### "Adopt a Family" Campaign
- Higher-income families can sponsor a low-income match.
- Sponsor gets NFT badge + public recognition + tax-receipt path later.

### Partner Merchant Ambassador Program
- Local businesses get early merchant spots.
- They promote FTC to their customers.
- They earn transaction fees + marketing support.

---

## 6. Influencer Targets

- Parenting creators with 50K–500K followers
- Crypto educators who focus on utility (not pump-and-dump)
- Minority financial literacy creators
- Local community leaders / pastors / coaches
- Micro-influencers over mega-influencers for trust

---

## 7. Launch Sequence

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1–2 | Landing page + waitlist live. Founder story video. |
| 3–4 | Twitter/X launch. Daily content. First 1,000 waitlist. |
| 5–6 | TikTok/Reels campaign. Parent testimonial videos. |
| 7–8 | Discord opens. Community contests. Referral rewards. |
| 9–10 | First merchant partner announcements. |
| 11–12 | Tokenomics deep-dive content. Audit announcement. |
| 13+ | Public sale / token launch. |

---

## 8. Metrics to Track

- Waitlist signups
- Referral invites sent
- Social followers (X, TikTok, Discord)
- Engagement rate on founder content
- Partner merchant LOIs signed
- Community-created content (UGC)
- Email open/click rates

---

## 9. Tone Rules

- No degenerate memecoin language.
- No guaranteed returns.
- No “to the moon” without context.
- Always lead with children, families, and real-world impact.
- Be a movement first, a token second.
