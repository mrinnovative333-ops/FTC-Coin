# Trading Mastery Study Plan

**Goal:** Learn what elite prop firm and forex traders do, then practice it until profitable on paper.  
**Timeline:** 90 days structured.  
**Style:** Day trading, swing trading, and forex.

---

## Phase 1: Foundations (Days 1–14)

### What to Study
- Market structure: support/resistance, trends, ranges
- Candlestick patterns and price action
- Order flow basics
- Risk management: 1% rule, R-multiples, position sizing
- Trading psychology: discipline, FOMO, revenge trading

### Recommended Sources
- **ICT (Inner Circle Trader)** — market structure, fair value gaps, order blocks
- **Al Brooks** — price action, bar-by-bar reading
- **Mark Douglas** — *Trading in the Zone* (psychology)
- **Andrew Aziz** — *How to Day Trade for a Living*
- **Adam Grimes** — *The Art and Science of Technical Analysis*
- **Forex:** No Nonsense Forex (NNFX), The Trading Channel (Steven Hart)

### Prop Firm Lessons
- Prop firms evaluate consistency, not big wins.
- Daily/weekly loss limits are the real test.
- Risk:reward minimum is usually 1:2 or better.
- Most traders fail because of psychology, not strategy.

---

## Phase 2: Strategy Development (Days 15–35)

### Core Strategies to Learn
1. **ICT Concepts**
   - Fair Value Gaps (FVG)
   - Order Blocks (OB)
   - Breaker Blocks
   - Liquidity sweeps / inducement
   - Killzones (NY, London, Asian sessions)

2. **Smart Money Concepts (SMC)**
   - Institutional order flow
   - Liquidity grabs
   - Mitigation of imbalances

3. **Price Action**
   - Trend continuation and reversal setups
   - Higher timeframe direction, lower timeframe entry
   - Confluence: support/resistance + trend + session + structure

4. **Forex Specifics**
   - Currency pair selection (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, XAU/USD)
   - Session timing (London, NY overlap)
   - Spread and slippage awareness
   - Fundamental drivers: interest rates, NFP, CPI

---

## Phase 3: Backtesting (Days 36–55)

### Backtesting Rules
- Pick ONE strategy.
- Test 100+ setups minimum.
- Log win rate, average R, max drawdown, profit factor.
- Only trade setups that pass: win rate ≥ 50%, profit factor ≥ 1.5.

### Tools
- **Forex:** TradingView replay mode + FXBlue
- **Futures/Prop Firms:** NinjaTrader / Tradovate replay
- **Data:** Historical data from broker or third party

---

## Phase 4: Paper Trading / Demo (Days 56–75)

### Demo Account Rules
- Trade exactly like it's real money.
- Use prop-firm-style rules:
  - Max daily loss: 3–5%
  - Max total drawdown: 10%
  - Profit target: 10% in 30 days
  - Minimum trading days: 10–15
- Journal every trade.

### Prop Firm Simulators
- FTMO demo / free trial
- MyForexFunds / The5ers evaluation-style practice
- Use their metrics to judge readiness.

---

## Phase 5: Evaluation and Refinement (Days 76–90)

### Questions to Answer
- Is my strategy profitable over 50+ demo trades?
- Can I follow my rules 100% of the time?
- Do I stop after hitting daily loss limit?
- Am I overtrading?

### Go Live Criteria
- 60+ day profitable demo track record
- Drawdown under prop-firm limits
- Clear, written trading plan
- Psychology checklist mastered

## Daily Routine for Traders

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Pre-market | Read overnight news, mark key levels |
| First hour | Trade only A+ setups during killzone |
| Midday | Review, journal, no chasing |
| Afternoon | Backtest / study |
| Evening | Daily review, stats update |

---

## Prop Firm Rules to Internalize

1. **Risk first.** Profits take care of themselves if risk is controlled.
2. **Consistency beats home runs.**
3. **Trade the plan, not your feelings.**
4. **One good trade a day is enough.**
5. **Preserve capital. Prop firms fail you for drawdown, not lack of profit.**

---

## Key Metrics to Track

- Win rate
- Average R per trade
- Profit factor
- Max drawdown
- Expectancy
- Risk of ruin
- Consecutive losses
- Best/worst day
