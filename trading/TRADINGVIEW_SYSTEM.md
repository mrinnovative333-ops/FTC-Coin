## What to Study on TradingView (Elite Trader Curriculum)

### Week 1–2: Market Structure
- Higher highs, higher lows (uptrend)
- Lower highs, lower lows (downtrend)
- Equal highs / equal lows (liquidity)

## TradingView Replay Mode Backtest Rules

1. Pick ONE pair and ONE timeframe.
2. Use 1H or 4H for bias, 15M for entry.
3. Mark your setup before clicking play.
4. Set stop loss and take profit before entering.
5. Log every trade — wins and losses.
6. Minimum 100 trades before going live.
